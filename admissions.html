Our Admissions Process (B.Tech)

We make our B.Tech admissions simple and transparent, following all guidelines set by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).

  1. 1

    Appear for TS EAMCET

    Students must appear for and qualify in the TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test).

  2. 2

    Attend Counselling

    Based on your EAMCET rank, you will be called for the official TS EAMCET web counselling. You must select Mumtaz College (MCET) as your college of choice.

  3. 3

    Seat Allotment

    Based on your rank and preferences, TSCHE will allot your seat.

  4. 4

    Report to College

    Once allotted, visit the Mumtaz College admissions office with your allotment letter and required documents to confirm your seat.

Eligibility Criteria

For B.Tech Programs

  • Passed 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Must have a valid rank in the TS EAMCET exam.

For MBA Program

  • Any graduate with a 3-year degree (B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, etc.).
  • Must have a valid rank in the TS ICET exam.
