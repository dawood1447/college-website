B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering

About the Program

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at Mumtaz College is designed to provide a deep understanding of the principles that drive modern computing. Our program places a strong emphasis on high-demand domains like software development, data science, and artificial intelligence, ensuring our students are at the forefront of technological innovation. Guided by highly qualified faculty with extensive industry and academic experience, the department's vision is to produce socially responsible engineers capable of solving complex global challenges through cutting-edge technical solutions.

We believe in a "learn by doing" approach. Our curriculum is deeply rooted in project-based learning, where students move beyond textbooks to build real-world applications in our advanced computing labs. From mastering full-stack development to implementing machine learning models, students graduate with a versatile skill set that includes problem-solving, critical thinking, and technical proficiency, making them highly competitive in the global IT job market.

Key Information

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Total Intake: 60 Seats
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM
  • Affiliation: JNTU, Hyderabad

Core Subjects & Curriculum

Students will master a range of foundational and advanced topics, including:

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Web Technologies
  • Software Engineering

Labs & Facilities

Our CSE department is equipped with multiple high-performance computer labs, a dedicated server room, and specialized labs for AI and cloud computing, all with the latest industry-standard software.

We provide a 1:1 computer-to-student ratio during lab sessions to ensure maximum hands-on practice and personalized learning.

Career Opportunities

Graduates from this program are prepared for high-demand roles, such as:

  • Software Developer
  • Data Analyst
  • Full-Stack Developer
  • Network Engineer
  • Database Administrator
  • QA Engineer
  • Cloud Architect
