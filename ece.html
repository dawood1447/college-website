B.Tech Electronics & Communication Engineering

About the Program

The B.Tech program in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at Mumtaz College is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical electronics and practical communication technologies. The curriculum provides a comprehensive understanding of electronic devices, circuit design, and signal processing, while diving deep into modern communication systems like wireless networking, satellite communication, and optical fiber technology.

Through our state-of-the-art labs and industry-aligned projects, students gain hands-on experience in designing and testing complex electronic systems. Whether it's working on microprocessors, developing embedded systems for robotics, or mastering VLSI design, our graduates leave with the technical expertise and creative problem-solving skills needed for careers in leading tech firms, telecom industries, and R&D organizations.

Key Information

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Total Intake: 30 Seats
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM
  • Affiliation: JNTU, Hyderabad

Core Subjects & Curriculum

Students will master a range of foundational and advanced electronics topics, including:

  • Electronic Devices & Circuits
  • Digital Logic Design
  • Electromagnetic Waves
  • Analog & Digital Communications
  • VLSI Design
  • Digital Signal Processing (DSP)
  • Antennas & Wave Propagation
  • Embedded Systems

Labs & Facilities

Our ECE department features specialized laboratories equipped with modern oscilloscopes, function generators, and spectrum analyzers.

Key facilities include the Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab, the Digital Signal Processing Lab, and a dedicated VLSI design suite featuring industry-standard simulation tools.

Career Opportunities

ECE graduates are highly valued across multiple sectors. Prominent career paths include:

  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Telecom Engineer
  • VLSI Design Engineer
  • Network Planning Engineer
  • Hardware Design Engineer
  • Control Systems Specialist
  • Robotics Engineer
