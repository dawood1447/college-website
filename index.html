About Mumtaz College, Hyderabad

Founded in 2008, Mumtaz College of Engineering and Technology in Hyderabad is affiliated with the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTUH) and approved by the AICTE.

Located in Malakpet, our 5-acre campus aims to cultivate talent in engineering and management, maintaining a focused learning environment with a total enrollment of 40 students (2025).
