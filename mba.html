Master of Business Administration (MBA)

About the Program

The MBA program at Mumtaz College is designed to develop visionary leaders capable of navigating the complexities of the global business environment. Our curriculum balances theoretical foundations with practical applications, focusing on strategic management, financial literacy, and organizational leadership. We aim to transform students into corporate-ready professionals who can drive growth and innovation.

Through case studies, seminars, and industry interactions, students gain a holistic perspective on business operations. The program offers various specializations to align with student career goals, ensuring they develop deep expertise in their chosen field while maintaining a broad understanding of administrative excellence.

Key Information

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Total Intake: 60 Seats
  • Eligibility: Any 3-Year Degree
  • Affiliation: JNTU, Hyderabad

Specializations & Core Subjects

Our MBA program offers dual specializations in high-demand corporate fields:

  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Strategic Management
  • Business Analytics
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Marketing Research

Seminars & Corporate Training

Rather than traditional labs, our MBA department utilizes "Business Simulation Labs" and seminar halls for soft skills training, mock interviews, and group discussions.

We emphasize real-world readiness through internship programs and frequent guest lectures from industry experts and corporate leaders.

Career Opportunities

An MBA from Mumtaz College opens doors to administrative and managerial roles across industries:

  • Business Development Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • HR Generalist / Manager
  • Marketing Executive
  • Operations Manager
  • Data Analyst
  • Supply Chain Coordinator
