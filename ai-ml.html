B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

About the Program

The B.Tech program in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is designed to prepare students for the rapidly evolving field of intelligent systems and data-driven technologies. The program focuses on building a strong foundation in computer science while introducing advanced concepts such as machine learning algorithms, neural networks, deep learning, and data analytics.

Students gain hands-on experience through practical labs, projects, and real-world case studies using tools such as Python and modern AI frameworks. Graduates of this program are well-equipped for careers in high-demand fields including data science, artificial intelligence development, machine learning engineering, and research-oriented roles in both industry and academia.

Key Information

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Total Intake: 60 Seats
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM
  • Affiliation: JNTU, Hyderabad

Core Subjects & Curriculum

Students will master a range of foundational and advanced topics, including:

  • Python for Data Science
  • Machine Learning Algorithms
  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Reinforcement Learning

Labs & Facilities

Our AI & ML labs feature high-performance GPUs, access to cloud computing platforms, and advanced robotics kits to provide students with the tools they need to build next-gen AI systems.

Career Opportunities

Graduates from this program are prepared for high-demand roles, such as:

  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI Research Scientist
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • NLP Engineer
